Welcome to the online Covid vaccince database tool!
Select one of the options on the navagation bar to get started



Application Functionality


Record a vaccination:

Upon inputting a valid OHIP number you will be prompted for your vaccination information which will then be added to the database. If you have not already been registered into the database you will be prompted to do so.


Vaccine Availability:

Choose a vaccine type to display all the vaccination sites that have (or will) offer that type of vaccine along with the total number of doses that have shipped to that site.


Patient Directory:

Choose a patient from the database and show their vaccination status -- ie. the patient's name, ohip number, the date the vaccine was given and the type of vaccine that was given.


Worker Directory:

Choose a clinic to show a listing of all workers that work at a the site. Their name and credentials will be displayed.
